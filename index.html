asteroids near earth

"asteroids near earth" is an interctive datavis project made with NASA's data on near-earth-objects.

based on the input date on the left, asteroids that were near the earth will be visualized.

each asteroid is shown with different randomized colors, and is moving according to its size and speed, measured and recorded by nasa.

all data is provided by NASA's open api: api.nasa.gov
